About Us

Technology can be your friend

These days, technology is becoming more important but also more complicated every day. This complexity drives away many people whose lives could be a lot more comfortable if they could use it. That's where we come in. We want to make technology easy to understand for everyone so that we can all enjoy its benefits.

We bring you relief from the headache that technology causes you. Technology surrounds us all and plays a very important part in society. This forces us to interact with it even when we do not want to, causing headaches and frustration. Whyte offers you a hand in dealing with this, making it more comfortable and less time consuming.

3 Rules At Whyte we use 3 basic rules to help make technology something helpful and enjoyable.

01

Stay curious

There's always more to learn, for everyone. Keeping a curious spirit can point us to mistakes and opportunities that are hidden away in life.

02

Everyone is an expert

A doctor is an expert in the human body. The valet is an expert in the cars that visit his lot. A mother is an expert in her child.
Everyone is an expert at something, learn from their expertise.

03

Do good work

When you do something, do it wholy and correctly. If only for one day all people would do that this world would become Heaven itself.
